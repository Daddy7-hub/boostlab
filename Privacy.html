2. What Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number — when you submit a contact form or reach out to us directly.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, time spent on pages — collected automatically through cookies and analytics tools.
  • Communication data: the content of your messages when you contact us via email, Telegram, or contact forms.

3. Purpose of Processing

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide requested information about our services.
  • To prepare project estimates and commercial proposals.
  • To improve our website performance and user experience.
  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns (anonymized).
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Consent: for cookies and analytics (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR).
  • Legitimate interest: for responding to inquiries, improving our services (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • Contractual necessity: for preparing project proposals (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR).

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device. We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: required for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: help us understand how visitors interact with our website. These are only set with your consent.

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie banner allowing you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics — for website traffic analysis (data may be transferred to the US under Standard Contractual Clauses).
  • Google Fonts — for web typography (no personal data stored).

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above. Contact form submissions are retained for up to 24 months. Analytics data is retained in anonymized form.

8. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — to request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to rectification — to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure — to request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restrict processing.
  • Right to data portability.
  • Right to object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected].

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our website uses HTTPS encryption.
